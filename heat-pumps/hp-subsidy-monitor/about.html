About HP Subsidy Monitor

A comprehensive dashboard tracking heat pump subsidies, energy prices, and market data across 29 European countries.

Purpose

HP Subsidy Monitor provides a centralized view of government subsidy programs for heat pumps and hybrid heating systems across Europe. It helps industry professionals, policy analysts, and consumers understand the current subsidy landscape, track policy changes, and compare conditions across countries.

What We Track

Technology scope:

  • AW — Air-to-Water heat pumps (residential <20kW, commercial 20–350kW)
  • LW — Liquid-to-Water / Ground-source / Brine-to-Water
  • EW — Exhaust Air heat pumps (with water distribution)
  • Hybrids — Heat pump + gas boiler systems (add-on and integrated)

Excluded: Air-to-Air reversible heat pumps (split systems, VRF). Where market data includes A2A units, this is clearly marked.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Maps: Leaflet.js + CARTO tiles + world-atlas TopoJSON
  • Charts: Chart.js
  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL, EU Frankfurt)
  • AI News Analysis: Groq API + Llama 3.3 70B
  • News Source: Google News RSS (real-time articles)
  • Automation: Vercel Cron Jobs (daily 08:00 CET)
  • Hosting: Vercel (auto-deploy from GitHub)
  • Domain: insight-map.com (Namecheap)

Methodology

Market data: National associations with hydronic-only data are preferred (BWP, Uniclima, SULPU, VWP, HPA, PORT PC, WPA, VPI, FWS). Where only total figures exist (incl. A2A), this is marked.

HP Penetration: installed base ÷ total households × 100%.

Energy prices: Eurostat (H1 2025) for EU. Ofgem for UK. GlobalPetrolPrices for non-EU.

Subsidies: Verified against official government portals. Max available amount shown.
